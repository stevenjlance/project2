CORE PRINCIPLES OF DESIGN

WHITESPACE

Keep it short. Keep it sweet. Make sure there is lots of room between content.

COLOR

While we all love a pop of color, stick to less abbrasive colors. Save those crazy colors for your next night on the town.

TEXT

Your cursive fonts are tacky and don't look good. Save them for your 3rd grade writing class.
